Bride and Groom

ANDREA

Born in Berkeley and raised in Orinda, Andrea fondly remembers much of her childhood being spent in the pool, on the basketball court, and with her family. To this day, her swim record at the Moraga Country Club from when she was 7 years old still stands! A planner to her very core, Andrea enjoys travel and has lived in and visited many corners of the world. When Andrea isn't planning your next party or vacation, you can probably find her tearing up the dance floor or talking about getting a kitten.

MATT

Imported from the great white north, Matt grew up in Toronto, studied engineering at the University of Waterloo, and promptly moved ot the Bay Area to pursue his technology career. Matt grew up playing basketball but he'll play any sport that gets him running around. With the help of Andrea's expert swim coaching, he has now caught the triathlon bug. Matt enjoys a strong IPA, some healthly competition, and also loves to travel. One day, he hopes to have a dog (but not before Andrea gets her cat).
