Bride and Groom

ANDREA

Born in Berkeley and raised in Orinda, Andrea fondly remembers much of her childhood being spent around Bay Area pools, basketball courts, and with her family. To this day, her swim record at the Moraga Country Club still stands from when she was 7 years old! A planner to her very core, Andrea enjoys travel and has lived in and visited many corners of the world. When Andrea isn't coordinating her next event at work, you can probably find her tearing up the dance floor or talking about getting a kitten.

MATT

Imported from the great white north, Matt grew up in Toronto, studied engineering at the University of Waterloo, and promptly moved to the Bay Area to pursue his technology career. Matt also grew up playing basketball, and he'll partake in any activity that gets him running around. Thanks to Andrea's influence, he most recently has caught the triathlon bug. Matt enjoys soaking in Californian culture with a strong IPA and some hip hop. One day, he hopes to have a dog (but not before Andrea gets her cat).

HOW WE MET

The happy couple first met on Andrea's birthday in the summer of 2012 at the bar Nola in downtown Palo Alto. Andrea was celebrating with a group of friends and Matt was with a co-worker, grabbing a drink after work.

Break Your Heart by Taio Cruz ft Ludacris was playing and Andrea was singing along (to the Luda part, of course). Matt's opener was to comment on how much Andrea must really like the song. Andrea wasn't particularly amused, but Matt persisted, and asked to participate in the birthday festivities presenting her with some Nola beads as a birthday gift. After some chatting and dancing, Andrea agreed to give Matt her number.

For their first date Andrea and Matt went to La Strada, an Italian restaurant in Palo Alto. The week following the date they spent almost every day together, and they've never looked back.

OUR ENGAGEMENT

It was the summer of 2015, and Andrea and Matt had just returned from a trip to Austria and Ireland. Andrea's birthday was fast approaching, and Matt informed her that this year he had a special romantic weekend planned. Andrea was very suspicious. Something was up.

On the weekend of Andrea's birthday, they travelled to Santa Cruz, where Matt insisted that they take a walk on the beach to check out the sea lions and grab lunch. At the very end of the pier, Andrea was surprised by a group of her friends and family who delivered a huge "Happy Birthday!" Matt then took Andrea's hand and told her there was a second piece to the surprise. Matt proposed, and Andrea said yes.

The group celebrated with a champagne toast at the Dream Inn lounge, some beach volleyball, and a night of dancing.
